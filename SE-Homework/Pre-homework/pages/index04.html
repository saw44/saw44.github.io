Getting to Know Me

I come from a beackground of health and wellness. My twenty year practice of massage therapy has been a source of comfort and joy. However, even good things have their end date and I am in this program to start on a new path. I am elated to be here. It's been a very trying time for many and as an advocate for self care, I would suggest everyone get massage. I would suggest Opal Center for massage therapy . They are both an endearing bunch and exceptional professionals in the field.



    Ways I Self-Care

  • Get plenty of sleep and at least 3 liters of water a day.
  • Dry brushing the skin followed by self massage to help clear lymph nodes and muscles.
  • Take any opportunity to move the body. I'm an advocate for dancing.
  • Remember that no situation lasts forever and it's best to just be attentive to the present moment.
